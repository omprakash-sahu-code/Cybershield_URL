Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

CyberShield is designed with privacy at its core. We do not store personal information. When you scan a URL, we only transmit the URL to the Google Safe Browsing API to determine its safety status. Scan history is stored locally in your browser only.

2. How We Use Data

URLs provided are used exclusively for security analysis. We do not maintain a permanent server-side log of your scans or share your scan history with third parties, other than the security providers required for the analysis itself.

3. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with the Google Safe Browsing API and Google Gemini AI. Your use of CyberShield is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Local Storage

We use browser localStorage only to remember your theme preference (Light/Dark mode) and to store your scan history locally on your device. No tracking cookies are used. Clearing your browser data will remove all stored information.

5. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at security@cybershield.org.
